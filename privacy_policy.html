Privacy Policy — DreamTower

Effective Date: October 9, 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to the DreamTower mobile application (the “Application”), developed by Vladimir Golovlev (the “Service Provider”) as an ad-supported service. The Application is provided “as is.”

1. Information Collection and Use

User Provided Information

The Application does not require registration or the direct submission of personal information to the Service Provider. If you contact the Service Provider voluntarily (e.g., by email), the information you provide will be used only to respond to your inquiry and will not be shared with third parties except as described in this policy.

Automatically Collected Information

The Application may automatically collect limited technical data, including:

  • Device type and model;
  • Operating system version;
  • App version and usage statistics (e.g., session duration, crashes, performance metrics);
  • Advertising identifiers (such as Advertising ID).

This information is collected in anonymized form and used to improve performance, stability, and user experience, and to display ads.

2. Third-Party Services

The Application uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you or your device. These services have their own privacy policies regarding data handling.

  • Yandex AppMetrica — analytics
  • Yandex Mobile Ads — advertising
  • Epic Online Services — online gameplay features
  • Google Sign-In — authentication for online features
  • Google Play Services — platform services

3. Epic Online Services and Google Sign-In

Certain online features of the Application are powered by Epic Online Services (EOS), which may require authentication using your Google account. When you log in, Epic Games and Google may process information such as your user ID, email, or display name to enable multiplayer or cloud functionality.

The Service Provider does not store or have direct access to your login credentials or Google account password. Data processing for these services is governed by the privacy policies of Epic Games and Google (links above).

4. Advertising and Consent

The Application displays advertisements provided by Yandex Mobile Ads. You can choose whether to receive personalized or non-personalized ads. You can also manage ad preferences in your device settings.

Anonymous technical data will still be collected to improve the Application even if you opt out of personalized advertising.

5. Children’s Privacy

The Application is available for children under 13 years of age. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children without parental consent.

Anonymous technical data (such as session duration, crashes, and device type) may be collected to improve gameplay.

Parents or guardians can manage their child's data or withdraw consent for data collection by contacting the Service Provider at vgolovlev-dev@yandex.ru.

6. Opt-Out Rights

You can stop all information collection by uninstalling the Application using your device’s standard uninstall process. You may also disable personalized advertising in your device settings.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Automatically collected data may be stored for up to 24 months in anonymized or aggregated form. User-provided information (e.g., support emails) is retained only as long as necessary to provide assistance.

If you wish to delete your data, please contact the Service Provider at vgolovlev-dev@yandex.ru. Requests will be handled within a reasonable time.

8. Security

The Service Provider takes reasonable physical, electronic, and procedural measures to protect information processed and maintained. However, please note that no security system is completely infallible.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page and the “Effective Date” will be updated. Continued use of the Application after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or data handling practices, please contact: vgolovlev-dev@yandex.ru

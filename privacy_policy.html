Privacy Policy — DreamTower

Effective Date: October 9, 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to the DreamTower mobile application (the “Application”), developed by Vladimir Golovlev (the “Service Provider”) as an ad-supported service. The Application is provided “as is.”

1. Information Collection and Use

The Application collects anonymous technical data (e.g., session duration, crashes, device type) via Yandex AppMetrica to improve gameplay. Your device’s Advertising ID (AD ID) may be shared with Yandex Mobile Ads to display ads. This information is not stored by the Service Provider.

2. Online Accounts and Google Sign-In

Some features require login via Epic Online Services (EOS) using your Google account. EOS and Google may process account information (e.g., email, display name, user ID) to enable online gameplay and multiplayer features. The Service Provider does not store or access your Google account credentials.

3. Advertising

Ads are provided by Yandex Mobile Ads. AD ID is used solely to serve ads and is not linked with other personal data. Personalized ads for children under 13 (U.S.) or under 16 (EU) are shown only with parental consent.

4. Children’s Privacy

While the Application is for all ages, parental consent is required for collecting personal data from children under 13 (U.S.) or under 16 (EU). Parents can disable the transfer of AD ID to Yandex Ads in the app settings.

5. User Rights (EU GDPR)

Even if the Service Provider does not store personal data, some data (AD ID, account info) is processed by third-party services:

  • Yandex Mobile Ads – AD ID for advertising
  • Epic Online Services / Google Sign-In – account data for online features

To exercise your GDPR rights (access, correction, deletion, restriction, data portability, withdraw consent), you may contact these services directly:

  • Yandex Privacy Policy
  • Epic Online Services Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

You can also contact the Service Provider for guidance at vgolovlev-dev@yandex.ru.

6. Data Retention

Anonymous technical data may be retained in anonymized or aggregated form for up to 24 months. User-submitted information (e.g., support emails) is retained only as long as necessary to respond.

7. Security

The Service Provider uses reasonable measures to protect data, but no system can guarantee complete security.

8. Changes to This Policy

Updates to this Privacy Policy will be posted with a revised Effective Date. Continued use of the Application constitutes acceptance of changes.

9. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or your rights, contact: vgolovlev-dev@yandex.ru
